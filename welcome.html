Common questions
Do I need to make an account or password?
No. Your name + your company = your identity. Type it once, the system remembers you.
What if I lose my loadbook bookmark?
Ask Dalmex (or your dispatcher) for a new link — they can look it up. All your loads stay safe.
Can other drivers see my loads?
No. Your loadbook is private to you. Only you and your dispatcher see your numbers.
Do I still need paper tickets?
For now, keep using paper as backup. Once you trust the digital record (usually after a few loads), you can stop.
